## Getting Started

### First Steps

1. **Open the Platform**
   - Navigate to the CITEDECK homepage
   - You'll see the conference overview with key information

2. **Explore Public Pages**
   - Browse without logging in
   - Access: Home, About, Call for Papers, Registration, Program, Resources, FAQ, Contact

3. **Login to Access Dashboard**
   - Click "Login / Register" in the top-right corner
   - Use demo credentials (see below)

## Login & Authentication

### Demo Accounts

#### Quick Login Method (Recommended)
1. Click "Login / Register" button
2. On the login page, click either demo account card:
   - **Administrator Card** (red) - For admin access
   - **Participant Card** (blue) - For participant access
3. Credentials auto-fill, click "Sign In"

## Glossary

- **Track** - Topic category for papers
- **Review** - Peer evaluation of paper
- **Submission** - Uploaded research paper
- **Registration** - Conference attendance signup
- **Session** - Scheduled presentation time
- **Proceedings** - Published conference papers
- **Keynote** - Main invited speaker
